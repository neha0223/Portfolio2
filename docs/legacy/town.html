Sunken Town is a speculative VR narrative that immerses players in a coastal community now submerged due to rising sea levels. Players explore an underwater town to uncover a family's history, revealing how climate change and inequitable access to resources shaped their fate. Built in Unity with 3D assets edited in Blender, the experience blends environmental storytelling with emotional, intergenerational themes.

Unity Shader and Terrain
In this walkthrough, you'll see how the underwater shader, plant life, and sandy terrain work together to bring Sunken Town to life. The custom underwater shader adds depth-based color fade and light scattering, creating the illusion of being submerged. Layered seaweed and aquatic plants add aging to the scene.

Additionally, a hand-sculpted sandy terrain in Blender provides a natural, uneven base that ties the entire environment together into a believable underwater world.
